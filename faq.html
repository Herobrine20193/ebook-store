FAQ & Help Center

How do I purchase an ebook?

Browse our collection, add books to your cart, and proceed to checkout. After payment, your ebooks will be available in your profile's Download Center.

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot password?" on the sign in page and follow the instructions to reset your password via email.

How do I add books to my wishlist?

Click the heart icon on any book card. View and manage your wishlist from the navigation bar.

How do I contact support?

Email us at support@ebookstore.com or use the contact form on the Contact page.

How do I subscribe to the newsletter?

Enter your email in the newsletter signup section on the homepage and click Subscribe.

How do I enable 2FA?

2FA is enabled during sign up for demo purposes. Enter the code sent to your email to complete registration.
